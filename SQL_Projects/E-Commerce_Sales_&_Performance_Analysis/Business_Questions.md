# Business Questions & KPI Framework

This document outlines the main business questions addressed in the **E-Commerce Sales Analysis** project and the KPIs used to answer them.

---

## 1️⃣ Overall Business Performance

**Objective:**  
Evaluate the overall performance of the business in terms of revenue, customers, and operational efficiency.

**Key KPIs:**
- Total Revenue
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Orders
- Total Unique Customers
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Average Revenue per User (ARPU)
- Average Shipping Time (Days)
- Average Products per Order
- Cancelled Orders Ratio
- Failed Transactions Ratio

---

## 2️⃣ Geographic (State) Performance Analysis

**Objective:**  
Understand how revenue, top products, and customer value vary across different states.

**Key KPIs:**
- Revenue Share % per State
- Total Revenue per State
- AOV per State
- ARPU per State
- Top Product Name per State
- Top Product Quantity Sold per State
- Top Category Name per State
- Top Category Quantity Sold per State
- Top Supplier Name per State
- Top Supplier Quantity Sold per State

---

## 3️⃣ Carrier Efficiency Analysis

**Objective:**  
Evaluate carriers' efficiency in terms of order fulfillment, cancellations, and shipping times.

**Key KPIs:**
- Total Shipments per Carrier
- Average Shipping Time per Carrier
- Delivery Variance per Carrier
- Delivered Shipments
- Cancelled Shipments Quantity
- Cancelled Shipments Percentage

---

## 4️⃣ Product & Category Performance Analysis

**Objective:**  
Identify best-performing products and categories in terms of sales, quantity sold, and supplier contribution.

**Product Level KPIs:**
- Total Revenue per Product
- Total Quantity Sold per Product
- Revenue Share % per Product

**Category Level KPIs:**
- Total Revenue per Category
- Total Quantity Sold per Category
- Revenue Share % per Category
- Top Supplier per Category
- Total Quantity Supplied by Top Supplier per Category

---

## 5️⃣ Customer Analysis

**Objective:**  
Identify the most valuable and active customers based on purchases, reviews, and RFM metrics.

**Key KPIs:**
- Total Purchased Revenue
- Total Quantity Purchased
- Total Orders Made
- Favorite Product Category
- Quantity Purchased from Favorite Category
- Average Rating Given
- RFM Metrics (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)

---

## 6️⃣ Supplier Analysis

**Objective:**  
Determine which suppliers contribute most to revenue, product quality, and order fulfillment reliability.

**Key KPIs:**
- Total Products Supplied
- Total Unique Customers
- Total Orders Fulfilled
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Revenue
- Average Selling Price
- Revenue Share %
